About GreenHaven

GreenHaven is a gardening website built to help readers improve their gardens with practical, easy-to-understand advice. The site focuses on plant care, seasonal routines, and useful growing tips that are approachable for beginners but still valuable for experienced gardeners.

The visual style is inspired by premium garden magazines, with a calm layout, soft natural colors, and clear sectioning. This makes the content pleasant to read and easier to navigate.

GreenHaven can grow into a full gardening publication with more guides, seasonal content, and topic-specific articles in the future.
